A Few Words About the College

AADIM NATIONAL COLLEGE is one of the finest urban colleges in Nepal established in 2071 located at the prime location of Kathmandu a walking distance from ring road nearby Chuchepati Petrol Pump. It is located in between big commercial complex KL TOWER & BHATBHATENI Supermarket. AADIM COLLEGE imparts the best available +2 program as per the curriculum and syllabus designed by National Examination Board (NEB), in Science, Management, Humanities, and Law. At the undergraduate level, the college offers 4 years of 8 semesters BCA (Bachelor of Computer Application) & BBS (Bachelor of Business Studies) degrees affiliated to TRIBHUVAN UNIVERSITY. Moreover, the college is going to Launch BSc(CSIT), BIM, BIT, BBM, BSW, and MBS program in the near future. At AADIM COLLEGE, we recognize that higher education is a sound investment in your future. Whether you want to gain an edge in the job market, enhance your promotion prospects or even chart a completely new career direction, we can help you achieve your ambitions.
